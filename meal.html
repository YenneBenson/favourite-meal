My favorite meal

My favorite meal is party jollof rice. It is of Nigerian origin, and one particular characteristic of this meal is the smokey flavour and taste that comes with firewood.

Party jollof rice recipe

Ingredient Measurement
Long grain rice 4 cups
Red bell pepper 3-4
Scotch bonnet pepper 2
Medium-sized tomatoes 3
Sachet tomato paste 2
Cooking oil 11 Tbsp
Beef/Chicken stock To taste
Large red onions 1
White pepper To taste
Ground ginger 1 tbsp
Salt To taste
Seasoning cubes To taste
Other spices To taste

Steps for preparation

  1. Blend your red bell pepper, scotch bonnet pepper, onions, garlic and ginger. Then, put into a pot and boil till all the water is absorbed, giving you a thick paste.
  2. Parboil your rice, and wash it.
  3. Add cooking oil to a pot. Saute your onions till it's fragrant and translucent.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir while frying.
  5. Add seasoning and spices, and stir.Add tomato blend,then, add a little beef/chicken stock, and stir.
  6. Add rice and stir till all the rice is coated with the stew.
  7. Cover your pot and allow to cook in medium heat.
  8. Check for doneness, garnish and serve.
